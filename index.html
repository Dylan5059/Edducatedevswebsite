About Educate Devs

Founded by passionate developers, Educate Devs was created to bridge the gap between learning to code and becoming a professional developer. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality education, mentorship, and opportunities in tech.

Our mission is to create an inclusive community where developers of all skill levels can learn, grow, and succeed together. Through collaborative projects, educational resources, and industry connections, we're building the next generation of tech innovators.
